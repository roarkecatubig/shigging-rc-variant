The Shigging Chef

v. standing next to a cook with the intentions of stealing their cooking techniques



Welcome to Shigging Chef, an app created to seamlessly find and store your favorite recipes along with instructional YouTube videos. Not seeing the exact video you’d like? You can use the optional buttons to advance forward and backwards. In the upper right hand corner of the app, you’ll see a button called “Your Shiggings”. Use this feature to see your logged searches and reload past shiggings. This app utilizes the YouTube API, Yumly API, the Bulma CSS Framework, the Firebase Database by Google, and the brains of four emerging web developers. Happy Shiggings!

The Shigging Chef by Caylene, Josh, Roarke, & Shane.
